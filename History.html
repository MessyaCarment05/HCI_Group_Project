December 29th, 1987
Enzio Dante built an Italian restaurant called Muto in Milan. At that time, he could only rent a small building. Enzio is assisted by his friend who is a former famous hotel chef named Gavino. At first, they only had pizza on the menu
August 03rd, 1999
This year, their business is threatened with bankruptcy due to the monetary crisis. They were very, very confused about how they were going to keep Muto from closing forever. Then Enzio's girlfriend, Aurora, gives money from her savings to help Muto not go bankrupt.
February 14th, 2017
In 2017, Muto finally opened branch businesses in Paris, the Netherlands, Japan, South Korea, Indonesia, Mexico, Canada, Thailand and China. On the Muto menu there are many menus ranging from pizza, pasta, dessert and drinks
November 21st, 1994
7 years later, they were finally able to buy a bigger place and add 3 employees. Besides, they started to add pasta in their menu Also they already have 2 other restaurants in Rome and Venice. Their business is growing rapidly
January 12nd, 2006
In 2006, Enzio passed away and Muto's business was continued by his wife and best friend, Aurora and Gavino. Muto restaurant already has many branch businesses in Italian cities and plans to open branch businesses outside Italy and the European continent.
